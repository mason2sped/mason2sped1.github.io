Earth's Cupcake

This piece was crafted from minerals, crystals and rocks from all around the world, earths cupcake was crafted in 9 months 27 days 9 hours and 13 minutes, it represents the spieces of humans and the beauty they have.

It is 12 feet in height

This piece was recently sold to the World Art Museum for $97,286,320.
